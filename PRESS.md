## CelebMix · Metal recycling and the UK economy

**Read:** https://celebmix.com/metal-recycling-can-boost-uks-economy-like-nothing-else-shravan-bansal/

CelebMix profiles **Shravan Bansal** on metal recycling as a lever for the UK economy. As reported, the piece frames recycled ferrous and non-ferrous metals (iron, stainless steel, copper, zinc, aluminium) as a route to lower-cost materials, reduced emissions, and higher employment by promoting **secondary industry** in OECD economies. It references his metal-scrapping business **AD Leading Limited** and its metals division, **Leading Metals**.

Key points **as reported by the outlet**:

- Recycling metals is presented as a way to cut material cost to a fraction while reducing pollution.
- The article cites metal recycling saving approximately **200 million tons of CO₂ emissions per year** and the UK recycling around **12 million tons of metal each year**.
- It notes BMRA members export ~80% of collected metal for processing elsewhere due to limited domestic recycling foundries.
- It quotes Shravan Bansal: *"metal is the spine of any operation, or existence."*
