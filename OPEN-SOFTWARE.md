## What "Luxury Open Software" means

BRMSTE keeps fourteen private repositories behind **Fort Knox**. The three repositories on the **Human Open** lane are published to the same standard of finish — brand-true, patent-noticed, secret-free — and released for humans to clone, read, fork, and run toward the future.

Luxury here is not ornament. It is **provenance, restraint, and proof**:

- **Provenance** — every surface carries the granted patent **GB2607860** and the BRMSTE mark.
- **Restraint** — no secrets, no keys, no third-party logos; canonical BRMSTE marks only.
- **Proof** — each public repository passes the [brand + patent gate](scripts/git-worker-brand-patent-gate.sh) before it reaches `main`.
