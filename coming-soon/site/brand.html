Primary logo

680 × 400 · SVG vector outlines · zero font dependency

The full brand lockup including the BRMSTE™ wordmark, tagline, and descriptor. Use for all formal communications, pitch decks, documents, and hero sections. All letterforms are Proxima Nova Bold glyph outlines — zero font dependency, identical rendering everywhere.

Wordmark

Gold on transparent · 286 × 70

Use the gold wordmark on dark backgrounds. Use the dark wordmark on light or white backgrounds. The ™ symbol must never be omitted, repositioned, or resized independently.

Icon & favicon

B monogram · 400 × 400 · Favicon 32 × 32

The B monogram is for app icons, favicons, profile pictures, and small-format applications. Minimum icon size: 24px. Minimum wordmark size: 80px wide.

Colour palette

Gold is the only accent colour. Never introduce additional brand colours. Gold must always be flat — no gradients.

  • BRMSTE Gold · #C9A94E — wordmark, CTAs, headings
  • Gold Dim · #8C7632 — labels, dividers, secondary accents
  • Gold Light · #E8D48B — hover states, highlights
  • BRMSTE White · #F5F2EB — body text on dark (warm, never #FFFFFF)
  • BRMSTE Black · #0A0A0A — backgrounds (never #000000)

WCAG contrast: Gold on Black 7.2:1 (AAA) · White on Black 18.1:1 (AAA)

Typography

Proxima Nova is the exclusive typeface for all BRMSTE™ communications. No other typeface may be substituted in branded materials.

Fallback stack: 'Proxima Nova', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif

  • Light (300) — headlines H1, H2, body text
  • Semibold (600) — section labels, card titles, emphasis
  • Bold (700) — wordmark SVG paths only. Never for body text.

All section labels are uppercase, Semibold (600), with 2–4px letter-spacing. Headlines are always Light (300) — this is the signature weight.
